Legal
Policies & Terms

1. Nature of Service

EMOT CARE is an emotional support and guidance platform. We are not a licensed insurance provider. Our plans offer access to counsellors, mentors, and resources — not financial insurance products.

⚠️ EMOT CARE is not a substitute for professional mental health treatment. If you are in crisis, please contact a licensed mental health professional or emergency services.

2. Eligibility

Our services are designed for individuals aged 14–28. Users under 18 require parental consent for enrollment. By using our platform, you confirm your eligibility.

3. Plan Usage

Each plan includes a defined number of sessions per month. Unused sessions do not carry over. Plans auto-renew monthly unless cancelled before the renewal date.

4. Confidentiality

All counselling interactions are confidential. Exceptions apply only when there is imminent risk to self or others, as required by applicable law.

📋 Data We Collect

We collect your name, email, phone number, age group, plan selection, and payment details (non-sensitive). Session content is never stored without your consent.

🔒 How We Use It

Your data is used to provide and improve our service, match you with the right counsellor, and send service communications. We never sell your data to third parties.

📌 Your emotional and session data is treated with the highest level of confidentiality and is never used for marketing.

👤 Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data at any time. Contact hello@emotcare.in for data requests. We will respond within 7 business days.
